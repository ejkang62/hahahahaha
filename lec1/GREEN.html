The Color Green



The color green affects us physically and mentally in several different ways. Green is soothing, relaxing, and youthful.
Green is a color that helps alleviate anxiety, depression, and nervousness. Green also brings with it a sense of hope, health,
adventure, and renewal, as well as self-control, compassion, and harmony. ]The green color is often used to indicate safety
in the advertising of drugs and medical products. Green is directly related to nature and energy, so it is also commonly used
to represent and promote ‘green’ products.
